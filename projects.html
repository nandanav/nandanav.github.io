OSCP Certification

I obtained my OSCP certification from Offensive Security in 2020. The certification exam was a 24-hour exam within which you had to infiltration and gain administrative access to five different machines. After the initial 24-hour period, you had an additional 24-hours to write and submit a report detailing the findings
